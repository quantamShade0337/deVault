Terms & Conditions
Effective January 1, 2025

1. Nature of Service

DevVault is a neutral online marketplace facilitating subscription-based access to digital assets. DevVault does not own, control, host, or guarantee any listed asset. All assets are provided by independent third-party owners ("Asset Owners") and accessed by subscribers at their own risk.

2. User Responsibility

Asset Owners warrant they legally own or control all listed assets. Subscribers accept assets may experience downtime or withdrawal at any time. Both parties agree to conduct transactions honestly and in good faith.

3. Trust Score System

+5 billing success · −10 report upheld · −15 early termination · −25 misrepresentation · −50 severe violation. A trust score below 30 restricts asset visibility. A score at or below 0 triggers an automatic account ban including all assets and subscriptions. Trust scores are non-transferable and cannot be purchased.

4. Abuse Protection

Users are subject to rate limits: 10 chat messages per minute, 5 asset creations per hour, 3 shop creations per hour. Accounts with 5 or more unresolved reports are automatically suspended. All abuse logic is server-enforced and cannot be bypassed client-side.

5. Appeals

Must be filed within 7 days of enforcement. Reviewed in 7–30 business days. One appeal per action. Decision is final. Filing false appeals will result in trust score penalties.

6. Limitation of Liability

DevVault's total liability is limited to fees paid in the preceding 3 months and excludes indirect or consequential damages. DevVault is not responsible for data loss, service interruption, or infrastructure failures by Asset Owners.

7. Chat & Communications

Messages are permanent, immutable, and cannot be edited or deleted. Rate limit: 10 per minute. Messages may be reviewed in connection with reported violations. System messages are automatically generated and constitute official platform communications.

8. Stripe & Payments

All payments are processed by Stripe. Subscriptions activate only after Stripe webhook confirmation. DevVault does not store card data. Refunds are handled case-by-case and are not guaranteed. Failed payments transition subscriptions to PAST_DUE status.

9. Mandatory Acceptance

Creating an account requires acceptance of these Terms and the Privacy Notice. Each subscription transaction requires renewed acceptance at time of purchase. Timestamps of acceptance are permanently logged.

10. Termination

DevVault may terminate or suspend accounts for violations of these Terms at any time. Asset Owners may not delete assets with active subscriptions. Shops with active assets may not be deleted without first removing all listed assets.

Privacy Notice
Effective January 1, 2025

Data Collected

Email address, hashed password, IP address, device metadata and user agent, Stripe payment metadata (no card data), asset metadata and access instructions, chat logs, reports and appeals logs, trust score history, and full audit event logs. Profile images and banners are stored in Firebase Storage.

Purpose

To facilitate subscriptions, prevent fraud, enforce the Terms of Service, calculate and update trust scores, display access credentials to authorized subscribers, and maintain overall platform integrity and security.

Data Retention

Active accounts retained indefinitely. Inactive accounts (no login in 24 months) deleted after 24 months with 30-day notice. Enforcement logs and audit trails retained for up to 5 years. Chat logs associated with active subscriptions retained for the subscription lifetime plus 12 months.

Your Rights

Access, correction, and deletion (where legally permitted and not restricted by active enforcement actions). Requests handled within 30 days. Export your full data package via Settings → Data Export.

Third Parties

Stripe (payment processing), Firebase / Google Cloud (authentication and file storage). No data is sold to or shared with advertising third parties.

Cookies

HTTP-only, secure authentication cookies only. No tracking, analytics, or advertising cookies. Session tokens are invalidated on sign-out and on ban/suspension enforcement.

Security

All data in transit is encrypted via TLS 1.2+. Database access is role-restricted. Firebase Admin SDK tokens are used for server-side authentication verification only. All admin actions are permanently audit-logged with admin ID and timestamp.
